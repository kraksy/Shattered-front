### overview:

- units gain experience after surviving battles.
- after killing specific number of units.
### how it works:

- survive 3 battles to gain 1 perk.
- choose from:
   - +1 armor
   - +1 movement

### why use this system?

- encourages players to value their units.
- adds strategic weight to deployments.
